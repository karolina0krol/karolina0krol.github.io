BIO
Born in 1994 in Warsaw, where she currently live and work. Graduated from the Wojciech Gerson High School of Fine Arts in 2013 and obtained a Master degree in Graphics from the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw in 2022.

Works mostly with graphic design, illustration and art books/zines. Employs both traditional drawing techniques and digital graphics to regularly creating designs that range from posters, book/music covers and promotional materials for social media, to editorial/book design, and a variety of a graphics, illustartions and artworks for an art and music related companies, galeries, labels and collectives. From time to time she prints her graphics in silkscreen or riso.
